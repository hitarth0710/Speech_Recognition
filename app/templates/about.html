What is VoiceScribe?

VoiceScribe is a powerful application that transforms your audio recordings into accurate text transcriptions using cutting-edge AI technology. Our platform leverages state-of-the-art speech recognition models to deliver high-quality transcriptions in seconds.

Whether you're a student, professional, or content creator, VoiceScribe simplifies the process of converting spoken content into written text, saving you time and effort.

Key Features

Multiple Audio Formats

Support for MP3, WAV, M4A and FLAC formats

AI-Powered

Uses Wav2Vec 2.0 AI model for accurate transcription

Secure Storage

Safely store and organize your transcriptions

Private & Secure

Your audio and transcriptions are kept private

Technology

VoiceScribe is built on Facebook AI Research's Wav2Vec 2.0, a cutting-edge speech recognition model that delivers exceptional accuracy through self-supervised learning.

Our Tech Stack:
Python Flask PyTorch Transformers SQLAlchemy Bootstrap
